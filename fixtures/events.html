Site Schedule

It has been the custom to list the dates of the trip and to have a campsite available for the night before the listed start date. For example, if there is a Joshua Tree trip Dec. 3-4, a Sat. and Sun. There is a campsite reserved for the nights of Dec. 2 and 3. Tahquitz campsites are an exception; usually only one night - Sat. night. The number of campsite nights are generally listed if a trip with reserved campsites is longer than two nights.

Campsite Reservations

Information about campsite locations and rules may be found on the reservation confirmation forms from the granting agency. Look for the reservation confirmation forms in the comment section of the event detail page.

Waivers

The trip leader must make sure everyone who camps with us has signed a waiver. This includes all guests and whoever may occupy the campsite, whether they are climbing or not.

Joshua Tree Campsites

We have a confirmed campsite in Joshua Tree where there is a listed campsite number. Members are encouraged to use the club’s reserved Joshua Tree campsites even when there is no trip leader listed for that trip. A trip leader usually comes forward at the last minute.
